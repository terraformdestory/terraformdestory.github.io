tmux notes

  • After entering ctrl+b you can then run a tmux command, or type : to get a tmux prompt.
  • detach = ctrl+b d
  • tmux ls
  • tmux a -t 3 (attach session 3)
  • tmux a #  ←- attach last session
  • tmux new -s [name of session]
  • tmux a -t [name of session]
  • Managing Panes
  • ctrl+b " (split a pane horizontally)
  • ctrl+b % (split pane vertically)
  • ctrl+b [arrow key] (move from pane to pane)
  • Resize Panes
  • ctrl+b :
  • resize-pane -D 2 (expand pane down two lines)
  • -U, -D, -L, -R (up down left right)
  • Customizing themes
  • .tmux.conf
  • Jim Myhrberg’s tmux-themepack repo: https://github.com/jimeh/tmux-themepack
  • tmux shortcuts & cheatsheet: https://gist.github.com/MohamedAlaa/2961058#tmux-shortcuts--cheatsheet
  • Enter scrolling mode = ctrl+b [
  • Synchronize panes
  • ctrl+b, :, setw synchronize-panes on
